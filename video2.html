Video Editing II

What is Video Editing II about?

Video Editing II is an advanced continuation of Video Editing I, allowing students to refine their skills and create more polished, professional-quality videos. Building on the fundamentals, this course explores advanced editing techniques, including color correction, sound design, motion graphics, and storytelling. Students work with industry-standard software on complex projects that challenge their creativity and technical abilities. By the end of the course, they become more proficient editors, developing their unique style and preparing for higher-level filmmaking or media production opportunities.

Adobe Premiere Pro

Adobe Premiere Pro is a professional video editing software used in filmmaking, television, and online content creation.

  • Essential Editing Tools – Students learn to cut and trim clips, add transitions, and synchronize audio.
  • Advanced Features – Includes color correction, motion graphics, special effects, and multi-camera editing.
  • User-Friendly Interface – Designed for both beginners and advanced users, making it accessible for students.
  • Industry Standard – Used by professionals worldwide, giving students real-world editing experience.
  • Creative Development – Helps students refine their technical skills and storytelling abilities.

Exciting Projects (Click on images)

The NJNG Project Commercial is a professionally crafted video highlighting New Jersey Natural Gas's commitment to sustainability, energy efficiency, and customer service. Through dynamic visuals and compelling storytelling, the commercial showcases NJNG's initiatives, such as clean energy solutions and community outreach programs. The video engages viewers with impactful messaging, reinforcing NJNG’s role in powering homes and businesses while promoting a greener future.

The "Protect Me With 3" Commercial Video Series focuses on the importance of getting vaccinated to safeguard individual health and the well-being of the community. Each video emphasizes the simple, yet powerful action of getting vaccinated as a step toward preventing illness and protecting loved ones. Through heartfelt testimonials and clear messaging, the series inspires viewers to take proactive steps for their health, ensuring a safer, healthier future for all.

The "Music Video Project" allows students to choose a song and create a visually compelling music video that showcases their creativity and teamwork. From selecting the perfect track to designing scenes and choreographing performances, students are immersed in the full process of music video production. This project not only hones their technical skills in filming and editing but also lets them express their artistic vision, culminating in a unique music video that reflects their personal style.
